YOUR DAILY ROUTINE • 12–18 MIN
1-4

THE HUB (VALLEY IV) – Red path loop

1. Teleport to Originium Byproduct Processing Plant → gather Kalkonyx (rare mineral) right nearby + optional Aerospace Materials from Recycling Station.

2. Teleport to Logistics Platform → quickly pick Kalkodendra flower.

3. Teleport to Southern Meadows → grab Kalkodendra flower as well.

4. Teleport to Old Water Plant area → optional Aerospace Materials from Recycling Station → walk down to fight the anomaly for Perfect Essence → finish with another rare Pink Bolete fungi spot.

5

ABURREY QUARRY – Stockpile Area

Teleport directly to Stockpile Area → jump down quickly and collect Kalkonyx (rare mineral).

6-7

ORIGINIUM SCIENCE PARK – Green route

6. Teleport to Route 5 → zipline or walk to the Depot node → issue a delivery (pack your own resources – do NOT accept delivery requests!).

Most deliveries route toward Kohl Plant. Optional stop at Recycling Station for Aerospace Materials (marked 6.5).

7. Arrive at Kohl Plant → complete delivery → optional deeper run for another Kalkonyx.

*Accepting delivery requests usually takes much longer.

8-9

POWER PLATEAU – Purple loop

8. Teleport near Evacuation Zone → collect rare flower Chrysodendra while moving toward the next point.

9. Reach Planned Construction Area → ~160 m walk to Recycling Station (Aerospace Materials) + another plant of Chrysodendra.

10-12

WULING CITY – Pink path

10. Teleport to Juhou Marsh → collect rare plant Cosmagaric.
Optional short detour east for rare flower Vitrodendra.

11. Teleport to Maintenance Zone → walk or zipline to collect rare flower False Aggela.

12. Teleport to Tianjing Courtyard → collect rare flower Blighted Jadeleaf.

13

OMV DIJIANG BASE – Kernel Sector

13. Teleport directly to Kernel Sector → open Reception Room Stock Redistribution Terminal UI.

14-16

MARKET + FRIENDS LIST

14–15. Valley IV Stock Market + Wuling Stock Market (same process for both):

  • Check every available stock item in the market.
  • Look for the one with the highest sell percentage at any of your friends.
  • Only buy if the sell rate is ≥100%.

Once you find the best one: sell the stock there. Repeat for the other market.

Buy low • Sell at highest % possible (100–600%+) = massive profit

16. While visiting the friend: Help with production (up to 5 times) + Exchange clues (up to 5 times).

Both grant credits. Spend them in the shop on Oroberyl, permits, etc.

17

OPERATOR CONTACT

(Access via OMV Dijiang Base – Kernel Sector)

Check for operators with a gift icon → summon them and give gifts to raise trust.

BASE ASSIGNMENTS VIEW

Check and optimize assignments daily for trust, levels, rare items and credits.
